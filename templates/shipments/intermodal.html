INTERMODAL

Intermodal freight transportation offers you flexible options in moving goods to your customers by truck, rail, barge, vessel and air. For those who ship more than 750 miles, shipping by truck and train or ship, rather than just over-the-road (OTR), can add value to your supply chain.

Reduce transportation costs by using intermodal shipping containers that fit into any mode and require no individual handling. Containers can be switched quickly from truck to train, or ship to any other combination, saving time and making carriers more efficient.
